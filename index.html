About me

Hi, I'm Ieti and I in the sunny town of Kawakawa, Northland. When I'm not making websites I'm spending time with family & friends. I also enjoy working on my 08 Ralliart and making automobile parts with my mig/tig welder

I first started learning web development around 2016. Because I grew up with a Immunodeficiency disorder, I learnt Web Development just after leaving school as a potential job that I'd also be able to do from home whenever I'm sick. In 2017 I decided to take my hobby to the next level and enrolled at Vision college in East Tamaki under their Web Design & Development. I graduated with a Diploma in Web Design & Development and have since been freelancing for small business and family websites. I'm now looking for a web developer role
